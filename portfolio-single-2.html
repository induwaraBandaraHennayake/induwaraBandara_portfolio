Food Waste Saver Mobile App project

Project Description

The Food Waste Saver app is a real-time platform designed to reduce food waste by connecting restaurants, bakeries, grocery stores, and households with people who need affordable or free food. Through the app, businesses and individuals can quickly list surplus food items before they expire, while nearby users can view available options on a map, reserve them, and arrange pickups. With features like live timers, filters, ratings, and notifications, the app ensures food safety, trust, and accessibility. By making surplus food easy to share, this solution not only helps reduce waste but also provides low-cost meals, supports communities, and benefits the environment.

Identified Problem

Every day, restaurants, bakeries, grocery stores, and even households throw away large amounts of edible food due to overproduction, short shelf life, or excess cooking. At the same time, many people in the same community face food insecurity and struggle to afford meals. This mismatch between food waste and food need highlights a real-world problem — there is no easy, real-time system that connects those who have surplus food with people who can make use of it before it goes to waste.

Solution

The Food Waste Saver app is a real-time mobile platform designed to reduce food waste by connecting restaurants, bakeries, grocery stores, and households with people looking for affordable or free food. Businesses and individuals can quickly post surplus food with details like photos, expiry time, and whether it’s free or discounted, while nearby users can view available food on a live feed or map, reserve it instantly, and get pickup directions. With features like filters, countdown timers, notifications, and trust ratings, the app ensures convenience, safety, and transparency. This solution not only helps reduce waste and save costs but also supports local communities and those facing food insecurity.
