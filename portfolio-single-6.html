Digital Document Submission and Review System Web App project

Project Description

This project is a digital document submission and review platform designed for businesses and service providers to manage client documents efficiently. The system allows clients to upload files, track their review status, and receive feedback, while staff members review submissions, add comments, request corrections, and approve documents. The platform ensures transparent communication, structured workflows, and real-time status tracking throughout the document review lifecycle.

Identified Problem

Many organizations rely on email or manual methods for document submission and review, leading to misplaced files, unclear approval status, delayed responses, and poor communication between clients and staff. Clients often lack visibility into document progress, while staff struggle to manage multiple submissions efficiently. This results in inefficiencies, errors, and reduced overall productivity.

Solution

The solution is a centralized web-based document management system that streamlines document submission, review, and approval processes. Clients can upload documents, monitor progress, and respond to correction requests, while staff can review files, update statuses, and communicate feedback in one platform. This approach improves transparency, reduces processing time, enhances collaboration, and eliminates dependency on manual, email-based workflows.
